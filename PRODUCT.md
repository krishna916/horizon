# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Single user (the creator). Context: Personal productivity, fast entry, execution of daily commitments.

## Product Purpose
Horizon is a personal productivity foundation focused on Action Management and Information Management. It provides a coherent system for capturing information quickly, reviewing it, managing actionable work, and executing daily commitments.

## Brand Personality
Assistive, objective, unobtrusive. It surfaces reality, earns complexity, and prefers facts over states.

## Anti-references
Complex PKM systems, extensive project management systems, teams/collaboration tools, and over-automation. Knowledge graphs, backlinks, analytics. 

## Design Principles
- Capture First (frictionless fast-entry)
- Content First (metadata and views support content)
- Opinionated Before Configurable (clear workflows before customization)
- Earn Complexity (assist, don't enforce)

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard web accessibility (keyboard navigable, appropriate contrast). No specific reduced motion or color blindness constraints noted, but general best practices apply.
